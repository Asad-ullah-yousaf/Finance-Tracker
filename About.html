What is a Finance Tracker?

A finance tracker is a tool or system used to monitor and manage income, expenses, savings, and investments. It helps individuals or businesses keep track of their financial transactions to improve budgeting, spending habits, and overall financial health.
Types of Finance Trackers
1. Spreadsheets – Google Sheets, Excel (manual tracking with formulas).
2. Mobile Apps – Mint, YNAB, PocketGuard, Personal Capital.
3. Web-Based Tools – Zoho Expense, QuickBooks, Wave Accounting.
4. Custom-built solutions – A personal finance tracker using JavaScript, Python, or React.


Features of a Good Finance Tracker
✅ Tracking – Salary, side hustle earnings, passive income.
✅ Expense Categorization – Food, rent, utilities, entertainment, etc.
✅ Budgeting Tools – Set limits for different spending categories.
✅ Savings & Goals – Track progress toward financial goals.
✅ Debt Tracking – Monitor loans, credit card balances, and payments.
✅ Reports & Insights – Charts, graphs, and summaries of spending trends.
